About the Retreat

For two days, the founders and leaders behind Viola Growth's portfolio step away from the build — to a place that sits 400 meters below sea level, where the desert falls into the Dead Sea.
No screens to chase, no decks to ship. Just the people building the next generation of companies, gathered in one of the most striking landscapes on Earth.

An inspirational guest speaker.
